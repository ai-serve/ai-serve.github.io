Dataset and training
  • The network was trained on a meaningfully sized publicly available set of images.
  • Several data augmentation techniques were used.
  • The employed classification learning algorithm strives to look for features that make "male" faces different from "female" ones. I.e., when the served result yields "female", it means that the neural net failed to recognize these distinct features. However, as all neural nets are a "black box" mechanism, nothing can be certain of it.
  • The dataset consisted of such images where the single human face takes up a great proportion of the image.
